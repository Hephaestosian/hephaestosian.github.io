Privacy Policy for Emotion Cards

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

Emotion Cards ("the App"), provided by Hephaestosian, values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with Google Play’s User Data Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Emotion Cards focuses on providing emotional insights while minimizing data collection:

  • Diary Entries (AI Processing): Content you write is used temporarily to generate AI-driven emotional responses. This data is deleted immediately after the response is generated and is NOT stored on our servers.
  • App Usage Analytics: We collect anonymized data (feature usage frequency, crash logs) to improve service quality. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • No Sensitive Data: We do not collect contacts, location, or financial information.

2. How We Use Information

  • AI Service Provision: To analyze your diary content and provide personalized emotional feedback.
  • Service Improvement: To optimize app performance based on anonymous usage patterns.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data used for AI processing is handled through secure, encrypted channels and is not retained by any third-party AI provider for training purposes.

4. Children’s Privacy

Emotion Cards complies with Google’s Children’s Privacy Policy. We do not knowingly collect PII from children. We ensure that no inappropriate ads or content are served to younger users within the App.

5. Data Security

Your data is secured using modern encryption technologies (HTTPS/TLS) during transmission. Since diary content is processed in memory and not saved to a database, the risk of data exposure is strictly minimized.
