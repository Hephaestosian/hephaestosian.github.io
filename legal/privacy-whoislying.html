Privacy Policy for WHO IS LYING?

Effective Date: March 12, 2026 (Updated for AdMob & App Store Compliance)

WHO IS LYING? ("the App"), provided by Hephaestosian, is a mobile game application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, ensuring compliance with Apple's and Google's privacy guidelines, including social authentication and advertising identifiers.

1. Information We Collect and Process

We process the following data to provide gaming services and display relevant advertisements:

Category Data Description Purpose & Legal Basis
Social Authentication Email address, Name, Apple ID, Google UID To create and manage your game account and sync progress across devices.
Game Progress Scores, levels, items, and achievement timestamps Core service functionality stored in Firebase.
Advertising & Analytics Advertising ID (IDFA/AAID), IP address, crash logs Used by Google AdMob for personalized ads (only with consent) and Firebase for app stability.

2. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We use trusted third-party SDKs that may collect data to enhance your experience and support the App via advertising:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use the device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to serve personalized ads. On iOS, this only occurs if you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.
  • Sign in with Apple & Google: Privacy-focused login options to manage your game profile.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure cloud storage and performance monitoring (Crashlytics).

3. Your Privacy Choices (iOS App Tracking)

In accordance with Apple's Guideline 5.1.2, we respect your choice regarding tracking. You can opt-out of ad personalization at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Delete Advertising ID

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is encrypted during transmission and at rest using Google Firebase infrastructure. We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Account → Delete Account within the App.

Note: Account deletion results in the immediate and irreversible destruction of all game progress and profile data.
