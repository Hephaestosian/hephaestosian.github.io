Privacy Policy for BodySignal

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

BodySignal ("the App"), provided by Hephaestosian, is a personal health tracking application designed to help users record pain signals and view health-related metrics. This policy explains how we process personal and health-related data in compliance with Google Play and Health Connect policies.

1. Information We Collect and Process

BodySignal processes the following categories of data:

Category Data Description Purpose & Storage
User Profile (Optional) Birth year, gender Stored in Firebase using an anonymous user ID
Pain Records Pain location, severity, notes, timestamps Stored locally and securely backed up to Firebase
Health Connect Data Steps, distance, calories burned, sleep data, exercise data, heart rate, blood pressure, blood glucose, oxygen saturation, respiratory rate, body measurements (weight, height, body fat, body temperature), nutrition and hydration data Read-only access, processed locally and optionally synced
Identifiers Anonymous application-generated user ID Used solely to associate data with the correct user
Important: BodySignal does NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any directly identifiable personal information.

2. Google Health Connect

BodySignal integrates with Google Health Connect to read health and activity data with the user's explicit consent. All accessed data is used only to provide personal insights alongside user-recorded pain history.

  • Health Connect data is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • All permissions are read-only and can be revoked at any time.
  • The App does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations.

3. Data Storage & Security

User data is stored locally on the device and securely backed up using Google Firebase. All data is encrypted during transmission and at rest.

4. Data Deletion & User Control

Users have full control over their data:

  • Pain records can be individually deleted from the History screen.
  • Account and all associated data can be permanently deleted via Settings → My Information → Delete Account.

Once deleted, data is permanently removed from Firebase and cannot be recovered.

5. Medical Disclaimer

BodySignal is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment instructions, or medication recommendations. All insights are informational only.
