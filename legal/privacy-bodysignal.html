Privacy Policy for BodySignal

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

BodySignal ("the App"), provided by Hephaestosian, is committed to protecting your health and personal data. This policy explains how we process information including Health Connect data and Firebase storage.

1. Information We Process

BodySignal processes the following categories of data to provide personalized health insights:

Category Specific Data Points Storage/Processing
User Profile Age, Gender Firebase (Encrypted)
Health Logs User-inputted pain records, symptoms, notes Firebase (Encrypted)
Health Connect Data Steps, Sleep, Heart Rate, Activity data Processed locally & Firebase
Identifiers Anonymous Instance ID, Advertising ID (ADID) Firebase / Google AdMob
Privacy First: We DO NOT collect or store your name, phone number, or email address. Your data is tied to an anonymous unique identifier.

2. Google Health Connect Integration

BodySignal requests access to Google Health Connect to read activity and vital data. This data is used solely to provide a holistic view of your health in relation to your pain logs.

  • We do not sell Health Connect data to third parties or advertising platforms.
  • Access to Health Connect is optional and governed by your explicit consent within the App.

3. Cloud Storage (Firebase)

To ensure your records are safely backed up and accessible, we use Google Firebase. All data sent to Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not have access to your health data in a personally identifiable format.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers:

  • Google Firebase: Data storage and analytics.
  • Google AdMob: To serve non-personalized or personalized advertisements.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored for as long as you use the App. You can request data deletion or delete your data directly within the App settings. Once deleted, data cannot be recovered from Firebase.
