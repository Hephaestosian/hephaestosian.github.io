Privacy Policy for BodySignal

Effective Date: January 31, 2026 (Updated)

BodySignal ("the App"), provided by Hephaestosian, is a personal health tracking application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services, including Google Sign-In, Firebase services, and Google AdMob.

1. Information We Collect and Process

BodySignal processes the following categories of data to provide and improve our services:

Category Data Description Purpose & Legal Basis
Authentication (Google Sign-In) Email address, Name, Profile picture URL, Google UID To create and manage your account, and synchronize data across devices.
User Profile Birth year, gender To provide personalized health insights.
Pain & Health Records Pain location, severity, quality, timing, notes, and timestamps Core service functionality; stored securely in Firebase.
Health Connect Data Steps, sleep, heart rate, body measurements, etc. Read-only access to provide integrated health metrics within the App.
Device & Usage Data Advertising ID, IP address, crash logs, performance metrics Provided via Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics for app improvement and advertising.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party SDKs to enhance your experience. These providers may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure data storage (Firestore), user authentication, and crash reporting (Crashlytics).
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used to analyze user behavior and app performance.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may use the device's advertising ID to show personalized ads.
  • Google Health Connect: Used to integrate activity and health data from other apps.

3. Google Health Connect & Data Minimization

Our use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the Health Connect Permissions policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We do not sell your health data to third parties.
  • Health data is used solely to provide user-facing features within the App.
  • You can revoke access to Health Connect at any time through your device settings.

4. Data Storage, Security & Retention

Your data is encrypted during transmission and while at rest using Google Firebase infrastructure. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your personal information:

  • Access & Export: You can view your data within the App.
  • Deletion: You may delete specific records in the History tab.
  • Account Termination: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → My Information → Delete Account.

Note: Upon account deletion, all data stored in Firebase is immediately and irreversibly destroyed.

6. Medical Disclaimer

BodySignal is a tracking tool and not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment plans, or professional advice. Always seek the advice of a physician for any medical concerns.
