Pain & Health Logging Tool

BodySignal Understand pain patterns over time

Pain is not just a moment — it’s a pattern. BodySignal helps you record pain clearly, connect it with daily health context, and understand recurring signals from your body over time.

What BodySignal Helps You Track

  • ● Pain Location & Intensity: Record where it hurts and how strong it feels.
  • ● Body Map Input: Front and back body maps for intuitive logging.
  • ● Pattern & Recurrence: Visual summaries of repeated pain over time.
  • ● Health Context: View pain alongside sleep, activity, and daily habits.
  • ● 30-Day Pain Heatmap: See concentration and frequency at a glance.
  • ● PDF Export: Generate summaries for personal reference or clinic visits.

Important Notice

BodySignal does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. It is designed for personal record-keeping and self-understanding, and does not replace professional medical care.
