Emotion Cards Feelings, one word at a time

A child who can name a feeling can handle it. Emotion Cards turns emotional vocabulary into something to collect and play with — cards to gather, six games to play, and a diary to keep.

What's Inside

  • ● Collect the cards: Every emotion with its own word, opposite and example.
  • ● Six games: Charades, matching, opposites, mind detective and more.
  • ● Diary & weekly report: See which feelings filled the week.
  • ● No server, no account: Everything stays on the device.

Emotional Safety

Hard feelings are never wrapped up in a cheerful tone. Sadness, fear and conflict are acknowledged carefully first — and when something serious comes up, the app gently points the child toward a trusted grown-up.
