What Makes It Different

📞

The child calls first

Laity texts; the child chooses to call. Speaking starts from curiosity, not from a task.

✍️

Spoken words become handwriting

What was said out loud is written by hand, in Korean and English, and gently corrected.

🔒

Laity's voice never leaves the device

Laity's voice is synthesized right on the device, so it never goes out to an external voice service. What is sent elsewhere is listed openly in the privacy policy.
